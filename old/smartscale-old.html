Capabilities

Everything Operators Need to Scale with Confidence

Demand Forecasting

AI models predict hourly session demand up to 7 days ahead with 94% accuracy — accounting for weather, events, and commuter patterns.

Auto-Scaling Ports

During predicted peaks, SmartScale automatically increases available ports and sends load-balancing commands to your charger hardware — no manual intervention.

Dynamic Pricing Engine

Automatically raise rates during peak demand and offer discounts off-peak — maximising revenue per kWh while keeping utilisation high and drivers happy.
