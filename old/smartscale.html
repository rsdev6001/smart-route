Platform Capabilities

Every decision, backed by data.

Demand Heatmapping

Pinpoints high-demand zones with no charger coverage using aggregated GPS and session data from SmartRoute. Know exactly where your next installation will have the highest utilisation before you break ground.

AutoScale

Automatically activates reserve charging units and redistributes load during predicted peak windows. Capacity management runs without operator intervention — so revenue never stalls when demand spikes.

Dynamic Pricing Engine

Adjusts per-kWh rates in real time based on demand levels, grid load, and time-of-day signals. Operators capture more revenue at peak while keeping off-peak pricing competitive to maintain utilisation.

Site Intelligence

Recommends optimal new installation locations with projected ROI, payback periods, and footfall scoring derived from live mobility data. Replaces months of manual survey work with a 48-hour analysis report.

Fleet Operator Dashboard

Gives B2B fleet managers advance slot booking, priority charging lanes, and unified bulk billing across all locations. Built for logistics operators who can't afford fragmented invoicing or unplanned downtime.

Grid Health Monitoring

Tracks local grid stress in real time and coordinates with energy providers to stagger load. Prevents costly demand-charge penalties and protects infrastructure from overload events during peak periods.
