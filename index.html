Features

Everything Your EV Needs on the Road

Real-Time Charger Availability

See live status of every charger on your route — available, occupied, or offline — before you arrive.

Plug Type Matching

Auto-detects your EV's connector type — CCS, CHAdeMO, Type 2, or Tesla — and filters chargers accordingly.

Range-Aware Routing

Input your current battery percentage and let SmartRoute factor in real range to plan stops perfectly.

Fastest vs. Cheapest Toggle

Prioritize speed or savings. Toggle between fastest-charge routing and lowest-cost route options instantly.

Offline Maps

Download your complete route and charger data ahead of time for areas with limited connectivity.

Fleet Mode

Manage routes for multiple EVs simultaneously. Assign drivers, track progress, and optimize fleet efficiency.
